About Emmanuel Church

Emmanuel Church has been serving the community since 2074 BS. We are a Bible-believing, Christ-centered congregation committed to sharing the love of God with all people.

Our mission is to make disciples of Jesus Christ through worship, fellowship, and service to our community.
